Our Partners

Advanced Composites

Stronger than steel and built to withstand fires, floods, and storms, our advanced composite poles set a new standard in infrastructure. With minimal maintenance and longer lifespans, they cut lifecycle costs and boost long-term ROI.

Solar Power + Battery Backup

Each pole generates solar energy and stores it in onboard batteries—keeping lights on during blackouts and emergencies. This local energy support helps keep power flowing to homes during power failures and high-demand events.

WiFi Hotspots

Each pole doubles as a WiFi hotspot, creating a reliable network that stays online even during outages. It keeps people connected—whether for everyday use or emergency response.

Curbside EV Charging

Our poles offer Level 2 curbside EV charging—bringing convenient, revenue-generating infrastructure to neighborhoods, campuses, and commercial districts. It’s a scalable way to accelerate EV adoption where people actually park.

Purpose-Built

We offer a set of standardized configurations optimized for different environments — residential streets, commercial zones, campuses, and transit corridors. This approach ensures local fit while keeping manufacturing and deployment scalable.
